Bali - The Island of Gods

A mesmerizing destination rich in culture, art, and natural beauty.

Explore the Rich History of Bali:

  • Prehistoric - Early human artifacts found in Bali suggest the region was inhabited since early prehistoric times.
  • 8th Century - The establishment of Hinduism, marked by the construction of the Mother Temple of Besakih.
  • 16th Century - Arrival of Dutch ships, marking the beginning of European influence.
  • 20th Century - Bali becomes a prime destination for international tourism, known for its highly developed arts.
